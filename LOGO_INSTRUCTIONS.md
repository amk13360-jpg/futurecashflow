# Logo Usage Instructions

## Official Variants

### 1) Landing + Login (Blue Wordmark)
Use for:
- Landing page (`app/page.tsx`)
- Admin login (`app/login/admin/page.tsx`)
- AP login (`app/login/ap/page.tsx`)
- AP change password (`app/login/ap/change-password/page.tsx`)
- Supplier access (`app/supplier/access/page.tsx`)

Use:
- `Logo` with `variant="blue"`
- Blue stays constant (icon, divider, and both words) and must not change with theme.

Example:
- `Future` bold, `Cashflow` normal
- Icon + divider = brand blue

### 2) Navigation Bars (Adaptive Wordmark)
Use for:
- Admin header (`components/admin/dashboard-header.tsx`)
- Supplier header (`components/supplier/supplier-header.tsx`)

Use:
- `Logo` with `variant="adaptive"`

Rules (nav/adaptive):
- Icon + divider: brand blue (fixed; does not change with theme)
- `Future` word: black on light, white on dark
- `Cashflow` word: black on light, white on dark
- `Future` bold, `Cashflow` normal

### 3) Forced Dark or Light
Only use when explicitly needed by design:
- `variant="dark"` = white wordmark + divider on dark/colored backgrounds
- `variant="light"` = dark wordmark on light backgrounds

## Component of Record
- `components/ui/logo.tsx`

## DO NOT
- Do not recreate the logo inline.
- Do not change logo colors per-page.
- Do not mix variants between landing/login and nav.
